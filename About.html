The History of Agip Oil Company

AGIP [Azienda General italiana petroli] is on italian oil Company founded in 1926. Here's a brief History

Early Years [1926-1945]

  • Founding:AGIP was founded on january 3, 1926 as a state-owned company to manage italy's oil interests.
  • Initial operations: AGIP began by importing and distributing oil products in italy.
  • Exploration and production:AGIP began exploring for oil and gas in italy and abroad including in NORTH African and the middel East.
  • sustainable efforts:Eni, including AGIP, has focused on sustainability, investing in renewable energy, reducing carbon emissions, and promoting energy efficiency.
